From Complexity to Clarity: Our Process

This project is ambitious, but its success is grounded in a clear, three-step process. We are not creating magic; we are building an intelligent pipeline that automates creativity. Here’s exactly how we'll do it.

1

Input & Analysis

Our Python backend receives the raw PIB text. It intelligently parses this document to identify and extract the most critical pieces of information—the key statistics, goals, and takeaways.

2

Translate & Enrich

Each key point is passed through our pre-trained OpenMT model for high-quality translation into Hindi and Tamil. The original and translated text are now ready for the final, creative step.

3

Animate & Render

The enriched data is fed into our Manim animation templates. The code programmatically generates scenes, renders them into a single video file, and presents it back to the user on our Streamlit interface.

The 10-Day Sprint to Launch

Phase 1: Foundation & Rapid Prototyping (Days 1-3)

Our focus is to build our core toolkit and prove our concept works by creating a minimum viable pipeline. This initial momentum is key.

  • Day 1 (Sun): **[The Visionary]** finalizes the first PIB scheme and begins creating Manim infographic templates. **[The Linguist]** sets up OpenMT models and a testable translation function.
  • Day 2 (Mon): **[The Visionary]** extracts key data points from the scheme. **[The Architect]** sets up the Python backend logic. **[The Artisan]** creates the initial Streamlit app skeleton.
  • Day 3 (Tue): **MILESTONE:** **[The Architect]** achieves a basic text-to-video render, proving our core animation pipeline works.

Phase 2: Integration & Core Build (Days 4-7)

We connect all our components into a seamless flow, building a fully functional application that can process real-world data.

  • Day 4 (Wed): **[The Architect]** connects the translation output to the animation pipeline. **[The Storyteller]** drafts the final 7-minute demo script.
  • Day 5 (Thu): All **[Full-Time Members]** collaborate on the end-to-end logic, parsing multiple key points from a full text block to generate a multi-scene video.
  • Day 6 (Fri): **MILESTONE:** First full video is rendered from a complete text block. **[The Artisan]** connects the Streamlit button to the backend and displays the video.
  • Day 7 (Sat): **[The Architect]** integrates performance metric tracking. **[The Storyteller]** begins recording screen captures of the working application.

Phase 3: Polish, Finalize & Document (Days 8-10)

The final push. We shift from building to perfecting, ensuring a flawless demo and professional documentation that tells a compelling story of innovation.

  • Day 8 (Sun): Stress-test the system with a second scheme. **[The Artisan]** adds final polish to the app's look, feel, and responsiveness.
  • Day 9 (Mon): Documentation day. All members contribute to the Tech Deck, Cost Sheet, and Roadmap, led by **[The Storyteller]**.
  • Day 10 (Tue): **FINAL REVIEW.** The entire team reviews all deliverables. The final 7-minute demo video is completed. We prepare for submission, 100% ready to win.
